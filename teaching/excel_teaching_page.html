What is Excel?

Microsoft Excel is a spreadsheet application used to organise, calculate, and visualise data. You use it to build everything from simple tables and charts to sophisticated financial models, dashboards, and automated workflows — making it indispensable for analysts, accountants, managers, and anyone who works with numbers.
