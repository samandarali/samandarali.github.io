    Model Evaluation & Validation

    Model evaluation measures how well a trained model performs on unseen data. Validation checks whether the model is reliable, stable, fair, and useful for the real business problem. A model is not good just because it has high accuracy; it must be tested with the right metric, the right validation design, and the right error analysis.
