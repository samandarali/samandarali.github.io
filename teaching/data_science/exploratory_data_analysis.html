    Exploratory Data Analysis Roadmap

    Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is the stage where you investigate the cleaned dataset to understand distributions, relationships, patterns, outliers, and possible explanations before formal modeling. It helps you decide what questions are worth answering, what features matter, and what problems still exist in the data.
