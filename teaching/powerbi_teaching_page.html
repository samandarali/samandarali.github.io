What is Power BI?

Power BI is a business analytics tool by Microsoft that lets you connect to hundreds of data sources, transform and model your data, and publish interactive dashboards and reports. It combines Power Query for data shaping, a data model for relationships, and DAX for calculations — making it the go-to platform for self-service and enterprise BI alike.
